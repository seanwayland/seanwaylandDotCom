CRUWEE COVE

These are unmastered mixes. Most of these files are uncompressed 48khz 24 bit mixes. They are as "loud" as made sense during mixdown. Via the browser you are listening to a down dither from 24 bit to 16 bit. Downloading and listening to the 24 bit files is highly encouraged by the artist as is making a donation here so I can at least cover the costs of streaming the audio to all.

Cruwee Cove ( Sean Wayland ). Drums : Gordon Rytmeister. Keyboards : Sean Wayland A song about an Australian indigenous person "Cruwee" watching the invaders arrive at the northern tip of Botany Bay. Drums recorded in Gordo's back shed August 2020.

Shane Howard ( Sean Wayland ). Drums : Nate Wood. Keyboards : Sean Wayland . Vocals : Guitar ( Arath Corral )Sean Wayland and Matilda Abraham. A song about the need for balance between right wing personal responsibility and "virtue" and compassion and togetherness. Drums recorded in Kaleidescope New Jersey june 2019 by Kyle Kassel

You know it ( Sean Wayland ). Drums : Nate Wood. Keyboards : Sean Wayland Drums recorded in Kaleidescope New Jersey june 2019 by Kyle Kassel

Summercloud Bay ( Sean Wayland ). Drums : Gordon Rytmeister. Keyboards : Sean Wayland. Bass : Alex Hewetson. Guitar : Oliver Thorpe Recorded at Oceanic studio by Jim Moginie feb 2019

Will we ever meet ( Sean Wayland ) . Gordon Rytmeister recorded drums in his backyard shed August 2020. The other tracks were recorded during my Barrenjoey album recording session.

Double drums ( Cole/Wayland) . Henry Cole double drums recorded in his apartment.Sean's synths recorded in his apartment april 2020. A real Covid 19 experience

I know ( Sean Wayland ). Drums : Gordon Rytmeister. Keyboards : Sean Wayland. Bass : Alex Hewetson. Guitar : Oliver Thorpe. Vocals : Matilda Abraham. A real old song of mine ( 1993 ?) searching for the right vocalist and band for 27 years. Recorded at Oceanic studio by Jim Moginie feb 2019
